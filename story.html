Before the shop, there was a trike.

Organic Man Coffee Trike began as a mobile coffee bar at city events in South Texas, serving espresso with a point of view: organic ingredients, cleaner roasting, and a cup that could take people somewhere.

How It Started

Built from movement, craft, and clean coffee.

The tricycle made its debut in 2015 at Laredo city events, creating a different kind of coffee experience: mobile, personal, and memorable.

In 2016, the team opened its first brick-and-mortar coffee shop and air roastery, bringing the same energy indoors while keeping the focus on organic ingredients and small-batch roasting.

Today, Organic Man Coffee Trike works with freshly roasted organic beans from selected fair-trade farms: certified non-GMO, single origin, and shaped by climates and soils from around the world.
